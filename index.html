How to Read My Work Via Email


HEWN (Hack Education Weekly Newsletter): I send this newsletter out once a week. Sent via Substack, this newsletter collects some of the week's most interesting/horrifying links in education and technology. Sign up here.

Hack Education RSS-to-Email: This newsletter, as the name suggests, sends a full copy of all new blog posts from Hack Education to your email inbox. Sent via Mailchimp, this newsletter is sent automatically every time Hack Education is updated. Sign up here. Note: as of August 2020, Mailchimp has closed my account because the company is stupid. This newsletter will be on hold until I find a new service.

Audreywatters.com RSS-to-Email: This newsletter, as the name suggests, sends a full copy of all new blog posts from my personal blog to your email inbox. Sent via Mailchimp, this newsletter is sent automatically once a week (on Mondays). Sign up here. Note: as of August 2020, Mailchimp has closed my account because the company is stupid and says the account was inactive, even though it regularly sends out rss-to-email newsletters. Jerks. This newsletter is now on hold.

I will not sell or share these mailing lists with anyone. Hell, I rarely look at who’s signed up or who’s un-enrolled because I don’t give a damn about analytics. (That being said, the services that operate these newsletters do have privacy policies that you should read.)
